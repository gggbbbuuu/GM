# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes the data practices of **AliveGR** (the "Software"). As an open-source project, transparency is a core value of this software.

## 1. Data Collection and Processing
The author of AliveGR **does not collect, store, or process** any personal data, identifying information, or usage statistics.
* **No Analytics:** There are no embedded tracking scripts or telemetry tools.
* **No User Accounts:** The software does not require registration or the submission of personal details.
* **No Usage Logs:** The author does not monitor how you use the software or what content you access.

## 2. Third-Party Domains and External Links
Due to its nature as an indexing tool and search engine, AliveGR connects to various **third-party public domains** and external web resources.

Please be advised that:
* **External Policies:** Once you access content via external links, you are subject to the Terms of Use and Privacy Policies of those specific third-party domains.
* **Lack of Control:** The author of AliveGR has no control over, and assumes no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.
* **Network Traffic:** While the software itself does not collect data, the third-party servers you connect to may see your IP address or other technical headers standard to web requests.

## 3. Local Configuration
Any settings or preferences changed within the Software are stored **locally on your device**. This information is not transmitted to the developer or any external server managed by the developer.
